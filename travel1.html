Cox's Bazar

The longest natural sea beach — waves, sunsets, and fresh seafood.

Why I Loved This Place

Cox's Bazar felt peaceful and energetic at the same time. Walking along the shore, watching the sunset, and trying local seafood made the trip unforgettable. I also enjoyed exploring nearby spots and taking photos during golden-hour lighting.

Nature Travel Photography

Top 5 Things To Do

  1. Walk on the beach at sunrise or sunset (best photos).
  2. Visit Himchari Waterfall and hill viewpoints.
  3. Explore Inani Beach with coral stones.
  4. Try local seafood like grilled fish and shrimp.
  5. Shop at local markets for souvenirs.

Quick Travel Info

Best Season November to February (cool and pleasant)
Budget Mid-range (hotel cost varies by season)
Transport Bus / Air to Cox's Bazar, local rickshaw or CNG
Food Seafood, Burmese snacks, coconut drinks
