Why I Loved This Place

Nature Travel Photography

Cox's Bazar felt peaceful and energetic at the same time. Walking along the shore, watching the sunset, and trying local seafood made the trip unforgettable.

I also enjoyed exploring nearby places, taking beach photographs, and experiencing the calm environment that makes Cox's Bazar one of the most special travel destinations in Bangladesh.

Top 5 Things To Do

1. Walk on the beach at sunrise or sunset for the best view.
2. Visit Himchari Waterfall and nearby hill viewpoints.
3. Explore Inani Beach and its beautiful coral stones.
4. Enjoy local seafood like grilled fish and shrimp.
5. Shop at local markets for souvenirs and small gifts.

Quick Travel Info

Best Season November to February (cool and pleasant)
Budget Mid-range (hotel cost varies by season)
Transport Bus or air to Cox's Bazar, then local rickshaw or CNG
Food Seafood, Burmese snacks, coconut drinks
